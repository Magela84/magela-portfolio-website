Cloud engineering with an automation-first mindset

I'm an Azure Cloud Engineer backed by nine years across enterprise IT and cloud engineering, now focused on designing, deploying, and securing cloud infrastructure. My work sits at the intersection of Infrastructure as Code, automated CI/CD pipelines, and container platforms on AKS — the plumbing that lets product teams move fast.
